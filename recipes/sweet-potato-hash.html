Sweet Potato Hash

Description:

This is a recipe I used to cook for a restaurant in Big Sky, MT. I have fond memories of this town (not of the restaurant), though I have ended up eating this at home with some regularity. Sweet potatoes are delicious, nutritious, and a great vehicle for old bay!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed and cut into slivers
  • 1 medium onion (yellow or red) diced
  • 2 cups arugula
  • olive oil
  • water
  • Old Bay
  • black pepper
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Cover cubed sweet potatoes in half inch of water and add salt to taste. Bring to a boil, cook until potatoes are cooked but firm. Strain and hold to the side.
  2. Heat olive oil in skillet and saute onions for about 30 seconds. Add cooked sweet potatoes and season.
  3. Saute until potatoes are starting to brown.
  4. Add garlic and saute for another 30 seconds
  5. Add arugula and saute until greens begin to wilt.
  6. Season generously with Old Bay.
  7. Serve with fresh fried eggs.

Crepes

Description:

Crepes are delicious and versitile french quick bread. Get creative with the filling, whatever you have on hand will work out great or put my suggestions on the shopping list.

Ingredients:

  • 1c AP flour
  • 1c milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 t salt
  • 2 T melted butter

Steps:

  1. Whisk together wet ingredients and add dries in and whisk until smooth
  2. Get a skillet to low heat and grease the bottom with butter
  3. With a small ladle, pour crepe batter into the center of the skillet and tip the skillet in a circular motion to coat the bottom of the pan completely
  4. Allow the batter to cook all the way before flipping
  5. Remove from the pan once the other side has just a little bit of color

Suggestions for filling:

  • Nutella and banana
  • Lemon juice and powdered sugar
  • Fresh berries

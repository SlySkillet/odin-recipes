Paloma

Description:

This is a favorite cocktail for the rotation. I highly suggest using mezcal if you can get your hands on it. This is a drink I started making in Oaxaca where the mezcal is cheap and plentiful. If that is not the case for you (I'm sorry) tequila will work just fine.

Ingredients:

  • 3 large grapefruits
  • grapefruit soda, preferably squirt
  • 3 oz mezcal
  • sal de gusano
